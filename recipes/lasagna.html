Lasagna

This lazy dump lasagna uses precooked mini meatballs and sausage, 3 cheeses, and dry pasta. You can mix it all together in a bowl, or mix it directly in the casserole dish. With no boiling required, it's a streamlined approach to a classic lasagna.

Ingredients

  • cooking spray
  • marinara sauce
  • beef bone broth
  • granulated garlic
  • cottage cheese

Steps

  1. preheat the oven to 350 degrees
  2. stir pasta, marinara and cantanzaro herbs
  3. tightly cover casserole with foil
  4. BAke in the oven for 50 minutes
